Welcome To Pearl Harbor

Hawaii has a rich history of involvement in world war 2. Since world war 2, there has been a large military presence in Hawaii. The historic attack on pearl harbor which got the United States involved in the war has a museum where one of the ships has sank. This location on the island is a popular tourist spot to learn about the history of the war.
