Welcome To Chinaman's Hat

On the beautiful island of Oahu in the state of Hawaii, there is a popular tourist destination known as Chinamans Hat. This beach park has spacious room for barbeques, relaxation, and swimming. At the beach park there is a not to distant island in the ocean that resembles what a hat looks like. Hence the name Chinamans Hat. This place is not only popular amoung tourist but also locals, and is nearby a famous valley where many movies are filmed.
