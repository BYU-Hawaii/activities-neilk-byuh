Some Food Found In Hawaii

In Hawaii you will find lot's of places to eat and foods to try. Recently in Hawaii ascai bowls has been appearing more. It's a tasty blend of fruits that one should try if in the area. Hawaii also has the Dole plantation. There they make lots of pineapple. In hawaii be sure to try some pineapple. A local favorite is the Loco Moco, which has rice topped with egg, a hamburger patty, and gravy. It is a popular item found at many plate lunch style resturants in the islands.
